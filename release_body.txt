We are excited to share this update! This release shifts focus to our core collaborative features and guarantees codebase stability.

### ✨ What's New
* **Community-First Experience:** The global, community-driven price reports screen is now the default landing tab upon opening the app. Easily navigate to your Shopping List as the second tab.
* **Seamless Back Navigation:** Pressing back from the Community Home screen now gracefully prompts to exit the application.

### 🔧 Under the Hood
* Restructured the `HomeNavHost` and `BottomNavigationBar` components for seamless tab transitions.
* Fixed desynchronized unit tests in the `ProfileViewModel` test suite, updating mocks to fully support the new use case and model architectures.
